What is NFT?

NFTs (Non-fungible tokens) are digital assets that are probably unique. Non-fungible tokens (NFTs) are cryptographically unique tokens that are linked to digital (and sometimes physical) content, providing proof of ownership. They have many use cases, including artwork, digital collectibles, music, and items in video games.
